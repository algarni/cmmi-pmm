Task Descriptor: Assess and Analyze Process Improvement Opportunities
This task is designed to maintain an understanding of the Processes strengths and weaknesses by assessing and analyzing process improvement opportunities.

Main Description

Process assessments are performed for the following reasons:

-        To identify processes that should be improved.

-        To confirm progress and make the benefits of process improvement visible.

-        To satisfy the needs of a customer relationship.

-        To motivate and facilitate buy-in.

-        To understand the driving forces.

-        To identify and document the objectives of Gap Analysis.

-        To define the scope of Gap Analysis

 
Steps
Step 1. Determine if an assessment or/and Gap Analysis for the whole process or any process area is needed
Step 2. Plan, Schedule, and Prepare for the Process Assessment (if an assessment will be conducted)
Step 3. Conduct the Process Assessment and document any process improvement opportunity
Step 4. Analyze all available process improvement opportunities
Step 5. Document the appropriate opportunities in PIP forms
Step 6. Prioritize the PIPs
