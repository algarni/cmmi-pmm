Main Description

The purpose of this task is not to describe the entire organization in details, instead it aims to allow the team to prioritize which parts of the organization to focus on for the upcoming improvement effort. The reasons for doing business modeling should guide the breadth and depth of the effort.

 

Based on the business-modeling objectives, the processes are prioritized, and the Process View of the Business Architecture Document is updated with the architecturally significant processes.
Steps

Step 2. Review Management Objectives

Step 4. Describe Forces and Objectives Affecting the Business Architecture
