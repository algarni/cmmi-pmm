Task Descriptor: Establish Process Improvement Action Plan
Identify improvements to the organization's processes and process assets and establish process improvement action plans to address improvements to the organization's processes and process assets.
Based on Method Task: Establish Process Improvement Action Plan

Main Description

The purpose of this task is to determine and prioritize the candidate process improvements as well as the process improvements that will be implemented.

This takes place by establishing and maintaining process improvement action plan which is targeting specific improvements that have been defined to address weaknesses uncovered by appraisals.
Steps
Step 1. Identify Process Improvement Objectives
Step 2. Identify Process Areas to be improved
Step 3. Identify Involved Organization Units
Step 4. Identify Resources for the Required Roles and Responsibilities
Step 5. Identify Potential Risks
Step 6. Prepare the Process Improvement Action Plan
Step 7. Review and Update the Lessons Learned
Step 8. Obtain Commitment to the Process Improvement Action Plan (Presentation , Meeting or Mail)
