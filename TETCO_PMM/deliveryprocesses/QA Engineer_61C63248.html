Main Description

The Quality Assurance Engineer must have an excellent understanding of software process management principles and must be skilled at estimation in an environment with incomplete or fuzzy information.

 

The responsibilities of the Quality Assurance Engineer can be detailed as follows:

1.   Objectively evaluating the PMM and PIM performed processes against applicable process descriptions, standards, and procedures.

2.   Objectively evaluating selected work products against applicable process descriptions, standards, and procedures.

3.   Communicating quality issues and ensures the resolution of noncompliance issues with the staff and managers.

4.     Establishing and maintaining review records of quality assurance activities.

5. Providing feedback to project staff and managers on the results of quality assurance activities
