Guideline: EPG Charter
Describe the RSLF_EPG Scope and Responsibilities
Main Description

Purpose

               EPG is a permanent committee and a focal point to improve the processes within the RSLF Business Solutions Departments to ensure that a high quality products are delivered to end users. The overall objective for  the EPG is to study and approve requests to  improve the effectiveness of all different software engineering processes PI, and PM in accordance with international standards. It supports the organizational capability framework to plan proposed process improvements requests, coordinate the implementation of those plans, and report the results to upper management.

Scope

               All Process Improvement Proposals (PIPs) that have been studied by the Process Engineers should be submitted to the EPG to take an action as following:

PIP Priority/ Type

EPG Action

Emergency or Low

EPG Leader Inform  the EPG members by email about the PIP and give them specific time  2-3 hours to respond with their comments then the PIP will be implemented

Assessment Reports

EPG Leader Submit to the EPG member  who is part of the affected department (assessed area) to study the report and give his comments which will be presented to the team in the regular meeting

Standard PIP

Scheduled to be discussed during the regular EPG meetings and need to take vote on whether or not approve the PIP

 

 

 

EPG Responsibilities

 

·        Provide Process Education & Training

·        Conduct  awareness sessions regarding different process areas that need to be improved

·        Study and take decision on PIPs that are submitted by Process Engineer

·        Follow up with the implementation of PIPs

·        Evaluate and monitor the usage of new processes, methods, tools, and technologies

·        Process Consultant to upper management

 

EPG Chair Responsibilities

 

·        Participates in EPG meetings to facilitate the exchange of perspectives between team members and manage the discussions

·        Prepare the regular meeting agenda and prioritize the list of PIPs that were submitted by the Process Engineer to be added in the agenda and discussed during the next meeting

·        Coordinate with the appropriate party if the submitted PIP is either with Emergency  or low priority, or an assessment report to be studied and obtain the result to present it the members

·        Ensure that all members participate in the discussion, share their opinions  and there is no dominant members

·        Prepare different kinds of reports to be submitted to the management regarding the EPG progress

·        Ensure that the EPG meetings are held in a regular basis and that all members are actively participating

·        Coordinate with the Process Engineers to follow up on the approved or rejected PIPs

 

EPG Member Responsibilities

 

·        Attend regular meetings effectively and be prepared by reviewing the agenda and understand the PIPs that will be discussed

·        Discuss and give his opinion in the possible impact or consequences on PIPs and suggest alternatives

·        Participate in train and educate others on processes

 

EPG Meeting Guidelines

·        EPG regular meeting is going to be held every other week for one hour

·        EPG Team Leader/ Process Engineer sends a meeting invitation to all members (and any other employee that SEPG need his participation/ opinion) along with the agenda by an Email message

·        Agenda contains all PIPs that are accepted by the CMMI Consult and are going to be discussed during the meeting

·        A vote is taken for any discussed PIP to be approved
