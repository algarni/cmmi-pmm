Role: PMO
This role leads the overall PMO effort. This includes quality and PMO advocacy, resource planning and management, and resolution of issues that impede the PMO effort

    Main Description

    This role is primarily responsible for negotiating the ongoing purpose and deliverables of the PMO effort, ensuring the appropriate planning and management of the PMO resources and assessing the progress and effectiveness of the PMO effort.

     The responsibilities of the PMO Manager can be detailed as follows:

    1.             Create and maintain Measures and reports for all departments

    2.             Distribute PMO tasks to PMO Team and monitor the work effort

    3.             Review and approve process improvement proposals

    4.             Review and approve the new versions of PI and PM

    5.             Establishing and maintaining procedures and Polices for the standard PIM and PMM after coordinate with related departments

    6.             Analyzing results of PMO activities against the defined criteria

    7.             Allocating and transferring resources across product groups and projects

    8.             Resolving issues affecting the PMO progress

    9.             Providing input to departments head on the PMO quality and PMO team skills, PMO process for continuous improvement

    10.      Identifying PMO policy deviation and analysis of risks associated with the deviations

    11.      Establishing smooth communication channels with external and internal customers

    12.      Promoting importance of meeting requirements

     
    Staffing

    Supported by the following basic skills and experience:

    -   General knowledge of all aspects of the software engineering process

    -   Experience in a wide variety of efforts, techniques and tools

    -   People skills, especially diplomacy and advocacy skills

    -   Planning and management skills

    -   Knowledge of the domain.
