Role: Process Engineer
The process engineer leads and coordinates software development process improvement effort by assessing current processes, studying and analyzing process improvement opportunities and extract the required buy-in and approvals by processes owners on implementing the proposed process improvement opportunities. The process engineer is responsible for monitoring and observing the software process and product quality and make sure that it meets the business and management objectives

    Staffing
    Skills

    A person acting as process engineer must be a good facilitator and have excellent communication skills. Knowledge of the software development business domain is essential for those acting in this role; however, it is not necessary for other roles. Process engineer should have an experience of different software development models and have the ability to analyze and identify the areas of improvement.

     

    A process engineer should be prepared to:

    -  assess the situation of the target organization where the project's end-product will be deployed

    -  understand customer and user requirements, their strategies, and their goals
