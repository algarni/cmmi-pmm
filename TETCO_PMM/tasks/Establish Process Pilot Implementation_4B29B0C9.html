Task: Establish Process Pilot Implementation

      Main Description

      Pilot projects are used to test out the solutions in both the process-centered and problem-centered approaches. The solutions will require some tailoring and refinement to fit them into projects across the development organization units, and the pilots will help determine the tailoring needs and guidelines for the rest of the organization.
